1. 关于Vim的替换操作

Replace All:

:%s/foo/bar/g
Find each occurrence of 'foo' (in all lines), and replace it with 'bar'.

For specific lines:

:6,10s/foo/bar/g
Change each 'foo' to 'bar' for all lines from line 6 to line 10 inclusive.
